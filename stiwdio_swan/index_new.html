What is Barret?

Barret is a 60-minute full-body workout inspired by the conditioning, alignment and discipline of ballet. It focuses on small, precise movements, working muscles that maybe you never even knew existed!

Why Barret?

Barret will:
  • Help you develop perfect posture  •
  • Improve your general level of fitness  •
  • Provide functional strength and mobility  •
